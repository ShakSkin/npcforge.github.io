The Lore System

What "Shared World Lore" Actually Means

Buying two characters separately gives you two isolated NPCs. Buying a bundle gives you two characters who exist in the same world and know it.

🏙️

One Shared Timeline

Every bundle includes a world lore document that establishes the shared history. The East Tower fire. The old king. The events that shaped every character in the pack. They all reference it consistently — because they lived through it.

🔀

Cross-Reference Memory System

Aldric's memory file includes entries about Dravek — what he thinks of him, what he suspects, what he won't say out loud. Dravek's file mirrors this from his angle. When players talk to both, the city feels inhabited instead of scripted.

🎭

Consistent Knowledge Boundaries

In a bundle, what each NPC knows and doesn't know is coordinated. Aldric doesn't know what Dravek privately thinks. Dravek doesn't know what Aldric reported to the watch. Senna knows things neither of them know they know.

⚙️

Coordinated API Config

Bundle configs are tuned together. Temperature, token budgets, and memory retrieval settings are designed so all characters in the pack respond at the same quality level without competing API costs.

Bundle Questions

Everything you need to know before buying.

Can I buy characters separately and get bundle lore later?

No. The shared lore document and cross-reference memories are built specifically for the bundle and require both characters to be set up together. Buy the bundle if you want the lore system.

When does Senna release?

Senna is in the Pro Pack as pre-release access. She will be delivered alongside Aldric and Dravek when you purchase the Pro Pack. Standalone Senna will be listed in the store separately once finalized.

Can I upgrade from City Bundle to Pro Pack?

Yes. Contact us after purchasing the City Bundle and we'll quote you the difference price for upgrading to the Pro Pack. You won't pay for Aldric and Dravek again.

Do bundle characters have to be used on the same server?

For the lore system to work properly, yes. The cross-reference memories are written assuming the characters coexist in the same world. They can technically be installed separately but the lore consistency requires both.

How is the bundle delivered?

As a single download — one folder containing both character packs, the shared lore document, and both install guides. Delivered to your email or Discord within 2 hours of payment confirmation.

What if I already own Aldric?

Contact us. We'll issue you a partial credit toward the City Bundle or Pro Pack. We don't want you paying for the same character twice.
