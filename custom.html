Your Input

What we need from you

The more you give us, the better the character. But even a short paragraph is enough to start.

📋 Required

  • ● Character role — What does this NPC do? Guard, merchant, healer, bartender, etc.
  • ● Personality tone — Gruff? Friendly? Suspicious? Cryptic? A few words is enough.
  • ● Server context — Medieval? Fantasy? Sci-fi? What kind of world is this NPC living in?

💡 Optional (but helpful)

  • ○ Backstory details — Any history, events, or relationships you want included.
  • ○ Speech examples — Lines you imagine them saying. Helps us nail the voice.
  • ○ Topics to avoid — Things this NPC should never discuss or always deflect.
  • ○ Relationships — Other NPCs or factions this character has opinions about.
  • ○ Secrets — Things the NPC knows but won't reveal easily.

Custom Order FAQ

How long does delivery take? +
Standard and Premium orders are delivered within 48 hours of payment confirmation. Elite (3-character group) orders are delivered within 72 hours. Rush delivery may be available — ask when you submit.
What if the character doesn't match what I wanted? +
Every tier includes revision rounds. Standard gets 1 round, Premium gets 3 rounds, Elite gets unlimited revisions. We work with you until the character feels right for your server.
Can I order a character type you already sell? +
Yes — but if you want a guard character and our Aldric pack fits your needs, buying from the store is cheaper. Custom orders make sense when you need a character tailored to your specific world, lore, or role that we don't currently offer.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cryptocurrency — Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, Solana, and many more. After we confirm your order details, we'll send you the wallet address and amount. Payment is handled before we begin building.
Can I use the custom character on multiple servers? +
Yes. Once you receive the files, they're yours. Use them on as many of your own servers as you like. You may not resell or redistribute the files.
Do you build characters for non-Minecraft projects? +
Our packs are optimized for SentiencePro and LlamaCraft Bridge. If you have a different AI NPC system with similar architecture (system prompt + memories + config), reach out and we can discuss. The personality engineering principles are transferable.
