Tell Me About Your Server

The more detail you provide, the better the character. There are no wrong answers — write like you're describing it to a friend.

💡 Tips for a Great Brief

  • Describe your world's tone — is it gritty, epic, comedic, mysterious?
  • Tell me what the character does day to day, not just their job title
  • Mention any other NPCs they interact with
  • Include any lore events that shaped them
  • Share example dialogue if you have it
  • Tell me what players will mostly ask them about

FAQ

Custom Commission Questions

Everything you need to know before submitting a brief.

When do I pay?

Not until you've confirmed the quote. After I reply with a final price and you agree, half is due upfront and half on delivery. Paying in full upfront gets a 5% discount.

What coins do you accept?

BTC, ETH, USDT, USDC, SOL, TRX, LTC, XRP, ADA, DOGE, XMR, BNB, BCH, XLM, POL, DASH, ZEC, DGB, and FEY.

How long does delivery take?

Single characters within 72 hours. World packs within 5 business days. Full systems within 7-10 days. Rush delivery (+$15) guarantees 24 hours for any tier.

What if I don't like the result?

One revision is included at every tier. You'll see the personality definition before final delivery so we can align on voice. If after revision it still doesn't match the brief, full crypto refund.

Can I use custom characters commercially?

Yes. Custom commissions include full commercial use rights. You own the character. Use it on any number of servers in your network.

Do you need anything specific from me?

Just the brief form. The more detail the better — world lore, character background, what players will ask, and example dialogue if you have it. I'll ask for anything missing.

Can I add characters to a world pack later?

Yes. Additional characters added to an existing world pack are quoted individually and built into the existing lore system at a reduced rate.

What's the difference between a custom commission and the Server License?

The Server License uses pre-built characters adapted for your server. A custom commission builds everything from scratch around your specific lore. Custom is always more tailored — and priced accordingly.
