Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install a character pack? +
Copy the character folder into /plugins/SentiencePro/personalities/ (or the equivalent LlamaCraft Bridge directory). Add your API key to config.yml. Restart your server. Assign the personality to your NPC. That's it — under 2 minutes.
What API do I need? +
You need an OpenAI or Anthropic API key. We recommend gpt-4o-mini for the best balance of quality and cost. Claude works great too. The packs include optimized settings for either provider.
Can I modify the personality files? +
Absolutely. The files are yours to customize. Change the character's name, adjust their backstory to match your world, add or remove memories. The pack gives you a professional foundation to build on.
How is payment handled? +
We accept cryptocurrency payments. After payment confirmation, you'll receive your download link. We support Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, Solana, and many other coins. All transactions are final.
What if I need a custom character? +
We build custom characters to your specifications. Visit the Custom Orders page for pricing and details. You describe your world, your NPC's role, and we deliver a full personality pack tailored to your server.
Do bundles include shared lore? +
Yes. Bundle characters are designed together. They reference the same events, know about each other, and have consistent world history. Aldric mentions Dravek's shop with suspicion. Dravek complains about Aldric's inspections. It's cohesive.
